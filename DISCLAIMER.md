# Disclaimer

## Not Legal or Regulatory Advice

This MCP server provides reference information about Brazilian sectoral regulations issued by ANATEL, ANS, ANVISA, ANEEL, and ANAC. **Nothing in this server constitutes legal, regulatory, or compliance advice.**

The information is compiled from publicly available official sources. It may be incomplete, outdated, or inaccurate. Brazilian sectoral regulations are amended frequently, and the data in this server is a point-in-time snapshot.

## No Substitute for Professional Guidance

Before making compliance decisions, product filings, certification applications, or incident reports based on information from this server:

1. **Consult the official regulatory portal** of the relevant agency (anatel.gov.br, ans.gov.br, anvisa.gov.br, aneel.gov.br, anac.gov.br)
2. **Engage qualified Brazilian legal counsel** with sector-specific expertise
3. **Verify current regulation status** directly with the issuing agency

Regulatory text is sourced from official Brazilian government publications. Each regulatory agency retains copyright and authority over its regulations. This server aggregates and indexes publicly available regulatory text for informational purposes only.

## Regulatory Scope

This server covers sectoral regulations only. It does not cover:

- General Brazilian legislation (see brazil-law-mcp)
- Brazilian data protection law (LGPD) (see brazil-law-mcp)
- Tax, labor, or environmental regulations
- State or municipal regulations
- Judicial decisions or administrative rulings

## Accuracy

While we make reasonable efforts to ensure accuracy, regulatory text may contain:

- Translation artifacts (Portuguese to English)
- Formatting differences from the original Diario Oficial publication
- Missing amendments, revocations, or consolidations
- Outdated provisions that have been superseded

Always verify against the official gazette (Diario Oficial da Uniao) for authoritative text.
